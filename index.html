My Trip To Japan

Tokyo


The Bright Lights of Shinjuku

My travels of Japan all stated in Tokyo. After a brutal 14 hour plane ride from St. Louis me and my friends arrived in Shinjuku where we would find our lives would never be the same. It only seemed that a sip of Saki was all it took begin our downward spiral in to degeneracy.

Mt. Fuji



After 4 long nights in Tokyo it was evident that our bodies and souls were in need of rest and relaxation. It was at this point that we took our ventures to the hallowed grounds of Mt. Fuji. Here we soaked in the ancient minerals and basked in the shadows of the volcano of lore. With engery renewed and our spirits higher than ever we found the strength and fortitude to continue our trek across The Land of the Rising Sun.

Osaka



With renewed vigor, we stepped off the bullet train to the famed city of Osaka. Little did we know what our first night would hold for us. The excitement and terror were equal in magnitude. While we got far more than we bargained for, we were not deterred in continued our explorations of this fabled city feasting on every type of delicacy we could get our hands on.

Kyoto



Kyoto was one of our final stops. This ancient city - once the capitol of Japan - would prove to be our salvation. Were it not for the fortunes of good karma bestowed upon us while in Kyoto I would have little confidence in my ability to retell the tales of my trip.
